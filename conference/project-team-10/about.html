About Boothly

Revolutionizing professional meetings with accessible, fully-equipped spaces wherever you need them.

Our Story

Boothly was born from a simple observation: professionals need quick, private meeting spaces, but traditional conference rooms are expensive, hard to book, and often unavailable when you need them most.

We envisioned a world where high-quality meeting spaces are as accessible as coffee shops, available in the places where people already spend their time - malls, business centers, airports, and transit hubs.

Today, Boothly provides thousands of professionals with instant access to premium meeting booths, enabling productive conversations anywhere, anytime.

Our Mission

Accessibility

Making professional meeting spaces accessible to everyone, everywhere, without the barriers of traditional office rentals.

Quality

Every booth meets our high standards for comfort, technology, and privacy to ensure productive meetings.

Innovation

Continuously evolving our technology and services to meet the changing needs of modern professionals.
