Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

You can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before your scheduled time through your dashboard or by contacting our support team.

What's included in each booth?

Every booth includes high-speed internet, AC, power outlets, comfortable seating, sound insulation, and a professional meeting environment.

How do I access my booked booth?

You'll receive a unique access code via email and SMS. Simply enter the code on the booth's keypad to unlock.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal for online payments, and cash payments on arrival at select locations.
