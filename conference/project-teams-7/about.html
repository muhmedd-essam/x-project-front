About Binova

Revolutionizing recycling through smart technology and community engagement.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, At Binova, we believe waste isn't worthless—it's potential. That's why we built a system that transforms everyday trash into rewards, habits into hope, and impact into a smarter, cleaner Egypt.

Binova is more than smart bins and an app—it's a movement. Turning Waste to Worth.

Vision & Mission

VISION: To reshape everyday waste habits in Egypt by turning small, wasteful objects into opportunities for environmental impact.

MISSION: To deploy a nationwide network of smart waste collection boxes linked to a mobile app that tracks user contributions, rewards sustainable behavior, and promotes a cleaner, more conscious Egypt.

The Name "Binova"

The name Binova carries our identity. It's inspired by the idea of turning something ordinary into something innovative—a name that reflects both simplicity and creativity, while leaving space for curiosity.
