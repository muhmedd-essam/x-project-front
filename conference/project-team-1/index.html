Advanced VR Headset

Our specially designed VR headset creates immersive social scenarios tailored for children with autism. Each experience is carefully crafted to gradually build confidence in real-world social interactions.

  • Child-friendly ergonomic design
  • Customizable social scenarios
  • Progressive difficulty levels
  • Safe and comfortable experience

Smart Monitoring Bracelet

The companion bracelet continuously monitors heart rate and stress levels, providing real-time feedback to ensure the child's comfort and safety during VR sessions.

  • Real-time heart rate monitoring
  • Stress level detection
  • Automatic session adjustments
  • Emergency alert system
